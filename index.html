    About True Tower

    True Tower ประกอบด้วยอาคารหลัก 2 แห่งคือ True Tower 1 และ True Tower 2

    True Tower 1

    True Tower 1 consists of 4 main buildings which are designed in modern, comfortable and applicable for multiple usages, including offices, shops, conference, training and seminar centers, along with a 10 meter high studio with indoor and outdoor parking spaces fitting more than 500 cars. This makes it one of the largest and modernized building in the potential business area of Pattanakarn Road near Pattakarn-Srinakarin Intersection and only 5 minutes to BTS Yellow Line Huamak Station

    About True Tower 1
    True Tower 2

    True Tower 2 consists of 4 main buildings which are designed in modern, comfortable and applicable for multiple usages, including offices, shops, conference, training and seminar centers, along with a 10 meter high studio with indoor and outdoor parking spaces fitting more than 500 cars. This makes it one of the largest and modernized building in the potential business area of Pattanakarn Road near Pattakarn-Srinakarin Intersection and only 5 minutes to BTS Yellow Line Huamak Station
